A little about us

LOCATED ON THE CALM PLACE OF AMBIKAPUR, A DELIGHTFUL EXPERIENCE

Welcome to The Kamoda Resort, the ultimate destination for relaxation and rejuvenation in Ambikapur. Our luxurious resort is located in a beautiful and serene setting, providing the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

In addition to the amenities and activities mentioned above, our resort also offers a variety of events and entertainment options to keep our guests entertained throughout their stay. From live music and performances to themed events and parties, there is always something fun and exciting happening at our resort.

We also understand that vacations are often a time to disconnect and relax, which is why we offer a variety of peaceful and serene spaces for our guests to unwind and recharge. Whether it's a quiet spot by a cozy corner in our gardens, there is a peaceful spot for everyone at our resort.

We invite you to come and discover all that the kamoda Resort has to offer. We are confident that you will leave feeling relaxed, rejuvenated, and refreshed, ready to take on the world with renewed energy and vitality.
